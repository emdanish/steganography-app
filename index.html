What is Steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing messages or information within other non-secret data or a physical object. In digital steganography, electronic communications may include steganographic coding inside of a transport layer, such as a document file, image file, program, or protocol.

How This App Works

This application uses LSB (Least Significant Bit) steganography to hide images within images. This technique works by replacing the least significant bits in the carrier image with the most significant bits from the secret image.
